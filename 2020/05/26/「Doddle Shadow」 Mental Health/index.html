    「Doddle Shadow」 Mental Health

    Posted on 2020-05-26 Edited on 2021-11-01

    Introduction

    The “Doddle Shadow” is a WeChat app dedicated to solving the psychological subhealth problems prevalent in our society, thus helping users to improve their self-awareness, explore their psychological potential and get out of the psychological haze.

    The main users of “Doddle Shadow” are people who are under high psychological stress and lack psychological treatment and self-reduction channels. “It is divided into three functional modules: psychological assessment, tree hole and spiritual station. The psychological assessment module provides users with a psychological assessment questionnaire to make a comprehensive assessment of the user and establish a psychological profile of the user to quickly help users understand their psychological status. The Tree Hole module provides an outlet for users to talk about their troubles, maximizing the release of their psychological pressure, while allowing them to receive encouragement and help from other users. The Mind Station module provides users with stress-reducing music as well as psychological articles, knowledge and therapies, so that users can conduct self-psychological guidance and self-stress reduction.
