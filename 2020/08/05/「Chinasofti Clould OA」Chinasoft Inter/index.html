「Chinasoft Clould OA」Chinasoft Inter

Posted on 2020-08-05 Edited on 2021-11-01

Overview

「Chinasoft Cloud OA」 is a cloud platform for the company’s internal network automation office, which integrates the functions of human resources such as employee management, business card information management, department management, function management, meeting management and approval, work schedule, cloud file storage, online information sending and receiving, leave process and reimbursement process approval, vehicle management, and outbound talent management. At the same time, the system realizes real-time analysis and visualization of some common data generated in daily work.

In the project development process, I am mainly responsible for the front-end part of five modules: personal center, announcement management, vehicle management, cloud file management, and schedule management, as well as the linkage testing between these five modules and the back-end. Among them, personal center consists of personal center, employee information maintenance, retrieving others’ information; announcement management; vehicle management consists of vehicle information, vehicle view, dispatch pending, dispatch record; cloud file management consists of cloud storage, sharing cloud files; schedule management consists of work arrangement, schedule, comprehensive query, calendar view, substitute reading setting, pending approval several sub-modules.

For the above modules, I am mainly responsible for rendering the data from the back-end to the front-end, using elementui and bootstrapvue two component libraries for front-end layout, unified front-end style style, each new, modify, view details of the business button pop-up style unified pop-up box, after saving, unified style style prompted the user processing results. When submitting the form, first verify whether the user input is reasonable, the main aspects are: whether the format of cell phone number, qq number and email is reasonable; whether the format of birthday, start date, end date and arrangement date is reasonable and the start date and end date cannot be later than the end date; and all the input contents that cannot be empty cannot be empty when submitting; the above submissions that do not meet the conditions will be organized in the The above non-compliant submissions will be organized in the front-end and prompted accordingly. Write form components that can cover the entire project, support search box, add, export, list, list operation as a single button, or drop-down menu, and pager, so as to ensure that after writing a component, in multiple pages, by passing parameters can control the components that appear in the page and the corresponding style.

Background

This system is an automatic office system used in the enterprise office. With the rapid development of global information technology, the traditional office has lagged behind the requirements of the development of information technology, and will be replaced by a new office. To build an internal enterprise information network and realize office automation (OA) has become an urgent requirement. OA brings us not only the ease of coping with office affairs, the realization of the entire office process computerized. More is a new office concept, is the inevitable requirements of the development of information-based society, is an infinite extension of our thinking; time and distance is no longer an obstacle to our access to information, will become an important part of our lives.

Functions

The system is divided into ten main processes:

1. Personal Center 2. System Management 3. Office Management 4. Meeting Management 5. Vehicle Management 6. Message Management 7. Approval Center 8. Outbound Business Management 9. System Announcement 10. Asset Management

Personal Center: After logging into the system, ordinary staff can enter the personal center page by clicking the personal center link, after which they can apply for leave, apply for car, apply for reimbursement, open personal folders, modify personal information, view personal business card folder and other operations.

System Management: After the system administrator logs in to the system, click System Management to enter the system management page. You can choose department management, role management, staff management and folder management for system maintenance.

Office Management: After logging into the system, ordinary staff can enter the personal office page by clicking the personal office connection. After that, you can choose to view personal to-do list, arrange others’ work, and view work arrangement authority. The office manager can then additionally perform maintenance operations on work arrangement permissions.

Meeting management: After logging in to the system, ordinary employees can select meeting management and enter the meeting management page. In the meeting management page, you can inquire about the meetings you are going to attend and the meeting applications you have sent. To initiate a meeting, you need to book a meeting room first, then enter the list of participants, and send the meeting information to each participant’s mailbox by email. Meeting approvers can additionally approve meeting applications, manage meeting rooms, manage meeting records and other operations.

Vehicle management: After clicking vehicle management to enter the vehicle management page after ordinary employees log in to the system, they can view and issue vehicle applications, view approval records and dispatch records, and export information reports. Fleet managers can additionally approve and delete vehicle applications, maintain approval records and dispatch records, and maintain vehicle information and other operations.

Message Management: After logging in, employees can click Message Management and enter the Message Management page to view messages in the Inbox, Outbox, Draft Box and Recycle Bin. Message super manager can additionally retrieve the message records sent by everyone in full text.

Approval Center: After employees with corresponding approval authority log in to the system, they can click Approval Center to jump to all corresponding approval pages and perform approval operations on the applications in the page.

Assignment Management: After logging into the system, the assignment manager can jump to the assignment management page by clicking Assignment Management, which includes the management of assigned employees, the management of partner companies and the management of assignment records.

System announcement: After logging into the system, ordinary employees can directly click the corresponding announcement title at the top scrolling announcement bar of the website to enter the announcement information viewing page, or click system announcement to enter the announcement viewing page. The announcement manager can additionally perform maintenance operations on the announcement information.

Asset management: After logging into the system, asset managers can see the asset management module in the left navigation bar, and can manage asset consumption and assets after clicking into it.

Related technologies

Back-end development framework: The project uses a three-tier architecture based on SpringBoot framework, SpringBoot is a lightweight open source framework based on the Spring Framework, which inherits Spring’s IOC (Inversion of Control) and AOP (Tangent-Oriented Programming) features, while simplifying the configuration process for developers in building the environment.

Big Data development framework: the project uses the Hadoop framework , the use of HDFS to achieve distributed storage of unstructured data , the use of MapReduce to achieve the mapping of unstructured data to structured data and data statistics , analysis .

In the front-end development framework: the project uses a front-end and back-end development, using Vue as the front-end development JavaScript framework, using elementui and bootstrapvue component library to complete the design of the page framework and effects.

Technical points involved

In terms of database development, we mainly involve technical points such as table design, full-text search, paging query, etc. In the process of designing database tables, we basically followed the third paradigm principle (i.e., there is no dependency of non-key fields on any candidate fields in the database tables), while for some query-intensive tables, we used the inverse paradigm design to improve the retrieval efficiency by adding some redundant fields. For full-text retrieval, we used the ngram full-text parser provided by Mysql to create indexes for full-text retrieval in some of the tables. For paging query, we used PageHelper, a MyBatis-based paging query plugin, to achieve paging information retrieval.

For back-end development, we used SpringBoot framework to achieve three-tier architecture development, maven to manage project dependencies, and MyBatis to achieve the Dao layer configuration and SQL file management. In the login management process, SpringBoot Security is used to achieve the management of login privileges. SpringBoot Scheduling is used to achieve the deployment of timed tasks.

In the front-end development, we used independent development, built a development environment based on Vue.js, used npm to manage the dependency files, and used iView to develop the page framework and page effects. In the front-end and back-end interaction, axios is used to achieve asynchronous interaction of front-end and back-end data. At the same time, we used the echarts plugin to display the dynamic data statistics on the page.

In terms of big data development, HDFS based on Hadoop framework is used to realize distributed storage of unstructured data, and MapReduce is used to realize structured mapping of unstructured data, as well as statistics and analysis. Meanwhile, fastdfs, a lightweight distributed storage system, is used for cloud file storage to achieve the management of employee cloud files. In the email filtering module, a Bayesian algorithm is used to classify word vectors and redis is used to store structured data sets to improve access efficiency.
